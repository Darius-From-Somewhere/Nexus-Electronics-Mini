History Of Nexus Corporation

In 1975, A man named Rudy 'Nexus' Carbine founded a company of the name Nexus. He had named it in that manner as he wanted to connect the world internationally with electronics. It started off as a simple company, before its own products range expanded into more practical electronics. In 2007, the company had decided to work on robotics as Mechanical Technology was growing. Fast forward to the modern day, it has managed to make several breakthroughs in robotics.
